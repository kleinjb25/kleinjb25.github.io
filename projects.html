Bell Bot

This was one of the games I helped program in Game Design Club. I was the lead on this project, so I was responsible for programming most of the logic (player movement and death, completing and failing orders, and more), level design, and combining all of the assets together. I worked with 15 other people, who helped program and design the UI, make music and 3D models, and write the order text, and made sure everyone did their part and that I was able to implement it into the game and credit them properly.
